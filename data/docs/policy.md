AI Product Policy

Acceptable Use Policy

Our AI platform is designed to assist users with information retrieval, question answering, and image understanding. By using this platform, users agree to use the system responsibly and respectfully.

Users must not use the platform to:
	•	Generate or share harmful, abusive, hateful, or violent content
	•	Generate vulgar, obscene, or inappropriate content
	•	Spread misinformation or fake news
	•	Attempt to hack, exploit, or misuse the system
	•	Upload illegal, copyrighted, or harmful images
	•	Use the AI for harassment, discrimination, or unethical purposes

Any misuse of the platform may result in restricted or blocked access.

⸻

Content Moderation Policy

To maintain a safe and professional environment:
	•	The AI system may filter or refuse to respond to harmful or inappropriate requests.
	•	Image uploads are analyzed and may be blocked if they contain unsafe or inappropriate content.
	•	Repeated policy violations may lead to permanent access restrictions.

Privacy Policy

We respect user privacy and data security.
	•	User queries and uploaded images are used only to generate responses.
	•	We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
	•	Data may be stored temporarily to improve system performance and accuracy.
	•	Sensitive personal information should not be shared with the AI.

⸻

AI Limitations Disclaimer

While our AI system uses advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Vision AI:
	•	The AI may sometimes generate incorrect or incomplete information.
	•	Responses should not be considered legal, medical, or financial advice.
	•	Users should verify important information from official sources.

Fair Use Policy

To ensure fair usage for all users:
	•	Excessive automated requests or spam usage is not allowed.
	•	The platform may apply rate limits to prevent system abuse.
	•	Commercial use may require special permission.
