FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What does this AI platform do?

Our platform is an AI-powered assistant that allows users to ask questions and get intelligent answers using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Users can also upload images, and the AI will generate captions and descriptive tags for the image.

⸻

2. How does the AI question answering work?

The system uses a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline. When a user asks a question, the system searches a knowledge base for relevant information and then uses AI to generate a clear and human-like answer based on that information.

⸻

3. What kind of images can I upload?

You can upload common image formats such as JPG, JPEG, and PNG. The AI will analyze the image and generate a caption and relevant tags describing the image.

⸻

4. Is the AI always accurate?

The AI provides intelligent responses, but it may sometimes generate incorrect or incomplete information. For important topics like medical, legal, or financial advice, please verify information from official sources.

⸻

5. Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your queries and images are only used to generate AI responses and improve system performance.

⸻

6. Is there a limit on how many questions or images I can submit?

To ensure fair usage and system performance, the platform may apply rate limits on excessive usage or automated requests.

⸻

7. Can I use this platform for business or commercial purposes?

Yes, but for high-volume or commercial use, you may need special access or permission depending on usage requirements.

⸻

8. What should I do if the AI gives a wrong answer?

If you receive an incorrect answer, try rephrasing your question or providing more details. AI responses improve when queries are more specific.

⸻

9. Do you offer customer support?

Yes, customer support is available to help with technical issues, account problems, or general questions related to the platform.

⸻
